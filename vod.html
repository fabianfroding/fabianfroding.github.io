Vault of Darkness

Warcraft 3 Mod
❮ ❯

A dungeon-crawler mod for Warcraft 3: The Frozen Throne (2003).

The mod allows 1-6 players to control of a group of heroes to descend into a dark desert dungeon. Players must collaborate to solve puzzles, and strategize to overcome coordination-demanding and reaction-based boss fights.

Main features include scripted enemy AI, dynamic hero selection system, a custom damage system, scripted custom abilities, custom items, custom UI, custom-made textures for all units, and much more.

The level design was heavily inspired by The Temple of Ahn'Qiraj from World of Warcraft, and Agon Wastes from Metroid Prime 2: Echoes. My main goal was to create a feeling of a desolate wasteland for the exterior, and a enigmatic atmosphere for the dungeon interior.

Tools Used:
• Warcraft 3 World Editor
• vJass (Warcraft 3's enhanced scripting language)
• War3 Model Editor (For model editing)
• MDLVIS (Also for model editing)
• MDLX Converter (For converting model formats)
• Krita (For texture editing)
• BLP Lab (For converting texture formats)
• MPQ Master (For extracting raw Warcraft 3 files)
